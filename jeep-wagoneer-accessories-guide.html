Jeep Wagoneer Accessories Guide 2026

The best accessories for your Jeep Wagoneer or Grand Wagoneer — ranked by protection, fit, and value. Updated May 2026.

Why Accessorize Your Jeep Wagoneer?

The Jeep Wagoneer and Grand Wagoneer are premium SUVs with a strong off-road heritage. Protecting your investment with quality accessories makes sense — especially if you use it for family trips, trail runs, or daily driving in tough conditions. The right accessories protect resale value and keep the interior looking new for years.

Top Accessories for the Jeep Wagoneer

1. Seat Covers — Premium Protection for Premium Seats

The Wagoneer's interior is one of its strongest selling points. Custom-fit seat covers protect the leather or fabric from spills, pet hair, mud, and UV damage. Look for covers that fit the Wagoneer's specific seat contours — universal covers won't do justice to this interior. Brands like Bartact specialize in custom-fit covers for Jeep vehicles.

2. Floor Mats — All-Weather Protection

All-weather floor mats are essential for any SUV used in real-world conditions. The Wagoneer's three-row interior means you need full coverage — front, second, and third row. WeatherTech and Husky Liners both offer Wagoneer-specific fitments with deep channels to contain water and mud.

3. Interior Accessories — Organize the Cabin

Center console organizers, cargo nets, and storage solutions keep the Wagoneer's spacious interior functional. With three rows and a large cargo area, organization accessories make a real difference on long trips.

4. Grand Wagoneer Accessories — Premium Upgrades

The Grand Wagoneer's higher trim levels deserve accessories that match the premium interior. Look for leather-compatible seat covers, premium floor liners, and tech accessories that complement the Grand Wagoneer's feature set.

5. Cargo Liner — Protect the Rear Cargo Area

A cargo liner is one of the most practical accessories for any SUV. It protects the cargo area from scratches, spills, and pet damage. Look for a liner that fits the Wagoneer's specific cargo dimensions and includes raised edges to contain spills.
